Andres 2007

Methods

Randomised trial

Participants

112 participants with type 2 diabetes (intervention 58; control 56)

144 community pharmacies in the province of Pontevedra

Spain

26 pharmacists

Year of study: February 2003 to March 2004.

Interventions

Drug knowledge was assessed by pharmacists using the "Dáder" method (a process for pharmacist follow‐up of patients who are receiving medication).

Compliance with medication was assessed using a modified Morisky‐Green questionnaire.

Every 3 months

Duration 12 months

Outcomes

HbA1C
