Methods

Randomised trial (by medical practice)

Participants

3074 patients on repeat medications (intervention 1614; control 1460)
Health professionals (delivering intervention): 62
Practices: 19

University‐affiliated setting
Medical practices in Grampian, United Kingdom
Unit of analysis mismatch corrected (randomised by practice, analysed by patient; analysis accounted for clustering effect)

Year of study: 1995 ‐ unclear.

Interventions

Pharmacist dispensed repeat prescriptions following a protocol to check whether items were required, or patients were experiencing side effects or drug interactions, vs usual care
Length of the intervention: not clear
Number of interventions: 12 during 12 months

Death rate

Hospital admissions
