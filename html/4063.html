Study characteristics

Methods

Parallel design

Multicentre study: not stated

Country: China
Follow‐up period: 5 years

Participants

  1. Elderly females with hypertensive emergencies and homocysteine (Hcy) (>10 mol/L)

  2. Randomised: 319

  1. High‐dose folic acid: 144

  2. Low‐dose folic acid: 136

Age: ≥ 65 year

Gender: only female.

Interventions

  1. Intervention: high‐dose folic acid: 0.8 mg/day

  2. Control: low‐dose folic acid: 0.4 mg/day

  3. Co‐intervention: salt restriction (≤5 g/day) and vitamin B12 500 μg/day.

Outcomes

  1. Ischaemic stroke

  2. Orthostatic hypotension
