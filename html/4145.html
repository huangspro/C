Hammad 2011

Methods

Randomised trial

Participants

199 patients with metabolic syndrome (intervention 112; control 90)

6 family medicine clinics at 1 university hospital

Amman, Jordan

Year of study: March 2009 to September 2009.

Interventions

Met with both pharmacist and physician. Pharmacists provided medication counselling, answered questions on self‐monitoring, lifestyle choices, compliance with drug therapy. Education materials were distributed discussing metabolic syndrome and increased risks. Monthly visits across 3 months

Outcomes

Systolic and diastolic blood pressure at 6 months

Fasting blood glucose (mg/dL)
