Year of study: Not stated.

Interventions

The intervention patients received a visit from the seamless care pharmacist (SCP) prior to the initiation of chemotherapy. The visit included medication history reconciliation. The SCP checked medication against established regimen protocols, including a drug interaction check, recalculation of the dose, and verification of pertinent laboratory values. The patient’s hospital pharmacist, oncology nurse, and attending physician received copies of the report. The SCP counselled the patient on their treatment, identified and resolved any drug‐related problems. Patients were followed up by phone 2 days post‐chemotherapy to identify/resolve drug‐related problems.
