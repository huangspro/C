Mahwi 2013

Methods

Randomised trial

Participants

Diabetic Centre

Sulaimany, Iraq

Year of study: September 2010 to January 2011.

Interventions

Pharmaceutical care. The intervention group was followed up for 3 visits. The interval between each visit ranged from 5 to 6 weeks with continuous weekly telephone calls for the follow‐up.
