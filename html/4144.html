Green 2008

Methods

Randomised 3‐armed trial

Participants

778 participants with hypertension: (Intervention (1) 202; intervention (2) 209; control 207

Setting is 10 primary care medical centres
USA

Year of study: Not stated.

Interventions

In the 2 intervention groups patients also received a self‐management support intervention (home blood pressure monitor and training and a web‐based service) in addition to usual care. In one of the intervention groups, a clinical pharmacist provided care management support by a single telephone call and subsequently the internet which provided a template for BP monitoring, current medication, a patient‐selected lifestyle goal, recommended medication changes and follow‐up plan. Communication thereafter was 2‐weekly by the web.

Duration: 12 months

Outcomes

Systolic blood pressure (BP)

Diastolic BP

Quality of Life
