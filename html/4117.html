Borenstein 2003a

Methods

Randomised trial

Participants

197 hypertensive patients (intervention; 98 control)

2 main offices of one medical practice of general internists and internal medicine sub‐specialists affiliated with a large community hospital

USA

Year of study: 1996 to 1998

Interventions

The intervention was made up of visits by pharmacist who assessed adherence to antihypertensive drugs, side effects, patient habits in accordance with guidelines as well as education about lifestyle modifications. Also follow‐up visits with physicians for treatment plans. On average there were 8 provider interactions over a 12‐month period.

Outcomes

Systolic blood pressure at 12 months

Number achieving blood pressure goals at 12 months
