Capoccia 2004

Methods

Randomised trial

Participants

Patients with depression: 74 (intervention 41; control 33)
Health professional (delivering intervention): 2
Practice: 1

University‐affiliated teaching clinic
Outpatient clinic in USA

Year of study: Not stated.

Interventions

Pharmacist collaborating with primary care physicians (PCPs) to provide patient education, antidepressant therapy adjustment, monitoring of adherence and adverse drug reactions and prevention of relapse, vs usual care
Length of the intervention: 15 minutes
Number of interventions: 13 during 12 months

Outcomes

Disease control using 20‐item Hopkins Symptom Checklist (SCL‐20)

Notes

Not all patients completed 13 sessions
55 patients in each arm required to detect a difference of 28% in clinical improvement rates at 0.05 significance level.

Funding source: Aetna Quality of Care Foundation

Conflict of interest: Not stated
