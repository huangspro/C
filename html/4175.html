Mazroui 2009

Methods

Randomised trial

Participants

240 diabetic patients (intervention 120; control 120)

1 hospital outpatient clinic

United Arab Emirates

Year of study: Not stated.

Interventions

Intervention patients were educated on their illness and medication needs, risk of complications, side effects and storage, healthy lifestyle, and self‐monitoring. They were also given a reinforcing leaflet of this information. 1 initial intervention contact with follow‐up assessments every 4 months for 1 year.

Outcomes

All measured at 12 months

Diastolic and systolic blood pressure (BP)

Fasting blood glucose mg/dL

HbA1c

Serum total cholesterol

SF‐36 (physical functioning)
