Kritikos 2007

Methods

Randomised trial

Participants

48 participants with asthma (intervention (1) 16; intervention (2) 16; control 16)

6 community pharmacies
Sydney, Australia

Year of study: January 2005 to July 2005

Interventions

Pharmacists delivered a single interactive Asthma Education Programme of 150 minutes to small groups of participants (5 – 8), focusing on asthma management, asthma medication, inhaler use. Relevant written information was also provided. Detailed programme guidelines, (which included the use of an educational resource kit Talk in A Box provided by the Asthma Foundation of New South Wales), were prepared to guide pharmacists through each session and enable standardised delivery of the programme.

Duration: Single session

Outcomes

Proportion with severe asthma, asthma quality of life
