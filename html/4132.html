De Castro 2006

Methods

Randomised trial

Participants

71 hypertensive patients (intervention 34; control:37)

Specialist clinic

Porto Alegre, Brazil

Year of study: Not stated.

Interventions

Intervention designed Dader method; obtain pharmacotherapeutic history, identify and challenge problems, and lifestyle changes to treat hypertension. Control received similar cognitive tests but focused only on drug‐related problems. 24‐week programme

Outcomes

Diastolic and systolic blood pressure (BP) at 4 months

24‐hour systolic BP at 24 weeks
