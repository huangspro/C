Lenaghan 2007

Methods

Randomised trial

Participants

136 elderly patients (intervention 69; control 67).

1 community pharmacist and patients from 1 general practice

Norfolk, England

Year of study: Not stated.

Interventions

2 home visits by a community pharmacist discussing drug interactions, education of medicines, removal of out‐of‐date drugs and assessment of need for adherence aid. Visits were arranged to include the carer of the elderly patient. Pharmacists discussed any issues with the general practitioner for possible changes to medication prescription.

Outcomes

Euroqol (EQ)‐5D

Hospital admissions

All‐cause mortality
